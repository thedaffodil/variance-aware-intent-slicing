VARIANCE ENHANCEMENT: TABOO OPENING WORDS
--------------------------------------------------

Avoid repeatedly starting intents with the following words:
- Create
- Update
- Set
- Increase
- Decrease
- List
- Delete
- Show
- Remove

Use varied and realistic opening patterns instead, such as:
- Traffic is getting worse around...
- The current slice seems overloaded...
- Can we check whether...
- After the event ended...
- Need more capacity for...
- The deployment is approaching its limit...
- The service quality is degrading...
- Operators are reporting congestion in...
- The temporary slice still appears active...

Do not generate repetitive command-style templates.

Prioritize lexical diversity and varied sentence openings.
